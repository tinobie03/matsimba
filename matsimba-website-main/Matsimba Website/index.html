About Us

Matsimba Technologies is a private company that designs, develops, deploys and maintains software and hardware systems

Our goal is to help you improve your business performance, optimize customer service, and tap in digital transformation

We partner with:

  • Government Ministries
  • Organisations
  • Industry Leaders
  • Civil Society
Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative software and hardware systems in Zimbabwe.

Mission

To provide intuitive, innovative, reliable and human centered software and hardware systems that contribute to the socio-economic progression of Zimbabwe and beyond.

Values
